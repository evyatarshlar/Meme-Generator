About

My project, MEME GENERATOR, is a tool designed to enable the automatic creation of memes in a simple and accessible way. After six intense weeks of learning software and design, I was able to develop a system for generating memes. The system accepts various images, texts, and styles, allowing users to create personalized memes quickly and efficiently. This project challenges personal creativity and provides a fun and engaging tool for everyday use.

Working on MEME GENERATOR was an exciting process, full of challenges and opportunities for creative expression. The combination of design principles, software development, and an intuitive user interface required me to learn and tackle a variety of new fields. I achieved significant improvement in my technical skills, and ultimately succeeded in reaching my goal: creating a simple and innovative tool that offers a unique experience to users. The collaborative and hands-on work with different technological tools made the process both fulfilling and enjoyable.
